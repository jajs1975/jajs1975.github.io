Openness for All

Enabling Sustainable Development

The Mexico 2015 Open Government Partnership Global Summit is a space that promotes productive dialogue and interaction between civil society representatives, government officials, reformers and practitioners from around the world.

The Mexico 2015 OGP Global Summit is the ideal scenario to discuss how open government practices enable the implementation of the Post-2015 Global Development Agenda, to be launched in September 2015.

What to expect

Participants

More than 1.500 participants from the social, private and public sectors sharing their experiences and knowledge as practitioners of open government.

Panels, round tables and working sessions

Panels, round tables and working sessions to discuss a variety of topics including:

Open Parliament, Open justice, Open Data, Privacy, Tech innovation, Health, Governance of natural resources, Open Policy, Open government at the subnational level and Reformer network.

Keynote speeches

Keynote speeches delivered by some of the most prominent figures in the open government ecosystem,including heads of state and leading civil society innovators.

Exhibit Area

A didactic exhibit area to showcase some of the most successful projects and innovations in open government.

Awards

2015 Open Government Awards gala to recognize and celebrate the initiatives that are helping to improve public service delivery.

Workshops

A variety of workshops to actively engage participants in a peer learning environment.
